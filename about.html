About the Hosts

Get to know the voices behind the Tapped In Podcast!

Jordan McCray

Jordan McCray, a proud Jersey City native, is a dedicated sports enthusiast and avid gamer with a deep-rooted passion for broadcasting and podcasting. As the former host of The Huddle podcast, Jordan established himself as a dynamic and engaging voice in sports media. Currently, he continues to share his insights and expertise as the creator and host of The JM Sports Show, a podcast where he delivers in-depth analysis, compelling discussions, and fresh perspectives on the latest in the sports world. Known for his authenticity and connection with audiences, Jordan brings his unique blend of enthusiasm and expertise to every episode, making him a standout in the podcasting landscape.

Diego Ruiz

Diego Ruiz, a creative mind from Jersey City, combines his passion for design and fashion with a drive for innovation. A former track athlete at St. Peter’s University, Diego's commitment to excellence extends beyond the track. His interest in fashion design reflects his eye for detail and a flair for artistic expression. When he’s not exploring the latest design trends, Diego enjoys cooking as a way to unwind and express his creativity in the kitchen. With a blend of discipline from his athletic background and a keen artistic vision, Diego is poised to make a mark in the world of design and fashion.
